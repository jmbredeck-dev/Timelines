# Custis & Lee Era

1778-12: John Parke Custis buys the property land. #custis
1802 / 1818: George Washington Parke Custis builds Arlington House. #custis
1831-06-30: Mary Custis marries Robert E. Lee here. #custis
1857: The Lee family inherits the plantation property. #custis

# Civil War & Aftermath

1861-04: Robert E. Lee departs for the Civil War. #war
1861-05: Union troops occupy the strategic house grounds. #war
1863: Federal government establishes Freedman's Village here. #war
1864: Government officially establishes Arlington National Cemetery. #war

# National Park Service Era

1933: National Park Service takes over house stewardship. #nps
1966-10-15: House listed on National Register of Historic Places. #nps
